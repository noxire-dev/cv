08規則

Do / Don't
DO
  • Hard shadows that shift. Offset blocks 4px on hover, no blur.
  • One accent per block at rest. Let it earn attention.
  • Black text on the accent highlight. Full-height block, maximum legibility.
  • Massive padding & Ma. Space is a design element.
  • Bilingual mono labels. Kanji + tracked caps.
  • Mechanical motion only. Snap, step, stamp.
DON'T
  • No border-radius. Ever. Sharp corners only.
  • No animated grain, rust or grunge. Weight comes from borders, not textures.
  • No soft / floaty easing. No springs, no parallax, no drift.
  • No second accent colour. One hue at a time.
  • No white text on the highlight. Low contrast — use ink.
  • No coloured-text emphasis. Highlight the background instead.

指示AI PROMPT · ZEN BRUTALISM v1.0 
Design in "Zen Brutalism" — a dark-only design language that fuses Japanese minimalism with industrial brutalism. Apply every rule below.

PALETTE (dark only, no light mode)
• Page #0C0C0D (warm near-black concrete). Panels #131316. Hover surface #191920.
• Text #ECECE4 (paper) primary, #93938C secondary, #55554F for meta/labels.
• Borders & grid lines #2A2A30 (#3E3E46 on hover).
• ONE accent colour, used sparingly: #8F90C2 iris (or #C86B53 terracotta / #7D8C6A wasabi). Only one at a time.

TYPOGRAPHY
• Display / headings: a wide industrial grotesque — Archivo Expanded (font-stretch 125%). Huge, tight tracking, uppercase for names.
• Body: Plus Jakarta Sans — clean, humanist.
• Labels / data / chrome: IBM Plex Mono, wide letter-spacing, uppercase.
• Signature: bilingual labels — a small kanji beside mono caps, e.g. "製品 PROJECTS", "現在 CURRENTLY".

STRUCTURE & SPACE
• 90° corners only — never border-radius.
• 2px borders on blocks; a 1px architectural frame around sections; optionally a faint exposed 12-column grid behind content.
• Massive padding and deliberate negative space (Ma). Asymmetric, weight-balanced layouts — anchor content off-centre, leave large voids.

MOTION (mechanical only)
• One easing: cubic-bezier(0.2, 0, 0, 1), ~0.13s.
• Hard, NON-BLURRING box-shadows that SHIFT on hover: translate the element -4px,-4px and grow an accent hard shadow (e.g. 9px 9px 0 accent). Never blur a shadow.
• No springs, floats, parallax or organic motion.

DETAILS
• Static concrete noise overlay ≤5% opacity, never animated. No rust, no grunge.
• A custom raw square cursor that tracks 1:1 and swells to a ring over interactive elements.
• Machine-plate numbering as quiet texture: PLT-001, P/03, SEC-04.
• Emphasis = highlighter: a solid accent block behind the words with DARK (#0C0C0D) text on top, like a marker. Never colour the text itself.
• One accent element per block at rest. Restraint over decoration.

Now build [describe your component or page], following this system faithfully.
